About Us

Uganda Martyrs' Secondary School Namugongo (UMSSN) is a Catholic-founded school in Namugongo, Uganda, known for its holistic education and Christian environment, aiming to develop students' academic and other talents, with a vision to be a center of holistic learning with a global impact.

Mission: To develop students' academic and other talents in a Christian environment.

Vision: To be a center of holistic learning with a global impact.
