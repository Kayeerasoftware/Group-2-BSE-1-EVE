About Us

Bunya Secondary School was founded in 1980 and has been a cornerstone of education in Mayuge District for over four decades.

Mission: To provide quality education that empowers students to excel academically and morally.

Vision: To be a leading educational institution in Uganda, fostering innovation and leadership.
